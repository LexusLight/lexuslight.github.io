# Lucky Weiss

*Original name: Лаки Вайс*

**Role:** S.M.D. captain · reformed antagonist

**Race:** Lilmotiit

**Source:** Golden Soul


A fox-eared captain of the Special Magic Department who once hunted the heroes and now fights beside them against the Green Duke. Wields Ether magic sharp enough to cut through reality itself — and jokingly calls himself the CEO of sadism.
