# Thin Green Duke

*Original name: Зелёный Герцог*

**Role:** antagonist · sparked the Cataclysm

**Race:** unknown

**Source:** Golden Soul


A revolutionary turned against the empire, provoking the fall of stars and the collision of two worlds. Wiped out an entire squad of legendary knights in one stroke — only the Candle, Scythe, and Cloud knights lived to tell it.
